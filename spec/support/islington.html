About Virgin Active

Everyone’s welcome at Virgin Active. Young or old. Fit or unfit. It’s all good.

We set out to create a health club that’s different from all the rest. We love helping our members to be active. But we’re not uptight or overly serious about it. That’s the way we do things around here.

We don’t believe in hidden extras or locked doors either – you can use everything you want to in our clubs.

So step inside and you’ll experience a health club that’s actively different from all the rest. You’ll be encouraged, inspired, and made to feel right at home.
